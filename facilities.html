Facilities & Amenities

World-class amenities crafted for the ultimate luxury experience.

Everything You Need

From our infinity pool to our private spa, every amenity is designed to exceed expectations.

Infinity Swimming Pool

Dive into our rooftop infinity pool that merges seamlessly with the city skyline. Open daily from 7 AM to 10 PM, the pool area features sun loungers, a pool bar, and attentive poolside service — perfect for a refreshing morning swim or a leisurely afternoon soak.

  • Open 7 AM – 10 PM daily
  • Heated year-round
  • Poolside cocktail bar
  • Towels & loungers provided

Luxury Spa & Wellness

Our award-winning spa offers a complete wellness retreat — from signature massages and body wraps to aromatherapy and hydrotherapy. A dedicated relaxation lounge and steam room complete the experience, leaving you completely revitalized.

  • 8 private treatment rooms
  • Steam room & sauna
  • Certified therapists on staff
  • Couple's packages available

Fitness Center

A state-of-the-art 24-hour gym equipped with cardio machines, free weights, and personal trainers available on request.

Fine Dining Restaurant

Savor an exquisite menu of international and local cuisine crafted by award-winning chefs, available from morning to late night.

Conference Hall

State-of-the-art conference facilities for up to 500 delegates, with AV equipment, high-speed internet, and dedicated event staff.

Airport Pickup

Complimentary luxury car transfers from and to the airport for all guests. Schedule your ride at any hour, seven days a week.

High-Speed WiFi

Complimentary gigabit-speed WiFi in every room, suite, lobby, pool area, and conference space — stay seamlessly connected throughout your stay.

Valet Parking

Secure underground parking with 24-hour CCTV and professional valet service. Your vehicle is in safe hands from the moment you arrive.

Laundry & Dry Clean

Same-day laundry and professional dry-cleaning services available daily. Complimentary pressing service for every guest on arrival.

24/7 Room Service

Around-the-clock in-room dining from our full à la carte menu. Cravings don't keep office hours — and neither do we.
